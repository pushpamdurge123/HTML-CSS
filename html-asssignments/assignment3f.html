Finding Nemo


A clown fish,is overly cautious with his son. Nemo, who has a foreshortened fin. When Nemo swims too close to the surface to prove himself,he is caught by a diver and horrified Marlin must set out to find him. A blue reef fish named Dory joins Marlin and complicate the the encounters with sharks, jellyfish and a host of ocean dangers. Meanwhile, Nemo plots his escape from a dentist's fish tank.
